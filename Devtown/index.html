Jishnu Pisharody

Music Producer, Mix Engineer & Student

The young prodigy and rising EDM producer Jishnu Pisharody aka JISTEON hails from Mumbai, India. Since a very young age he was fond of music and would always be mesmerized with how certain sounds would provoke certain emotions within. His interest in EDM was born when one of his classmates made him listen to "Scared To Be Lonely" by Martin Garrix. Jishnu was immediately surprised by the way the song was composed and started looking at the ins and outs of music production. He has since never looked back.

He mentions his influences to be Martin Garrix, Nicky Romero, Blasterjaxx, W&W, Tritonal and many more. His music is heavily dependent on powerful melodies and ambiences, combined by the rave and club style drops by which he aims to be the next big headline act.
